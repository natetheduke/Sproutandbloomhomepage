How to Start Composting

Start composting by layering brown and green materials, keeping it moist, and turning it weekly. Open piles, tumblers, and worm bins are great beginner options.

Beginner's Fertilization Guide

Use compost tea for quick absorption, granular fertilizer for slow release, and fish emulsion for organic strength. These can work together to enrich your garden soil.
